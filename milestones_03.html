Week 3 February 5, 2014

Question 1:

What area do you want to depict in your map? Why?

My map is focused on the southernmost half of the continental US, where I have gone to school and been employed. I used a method that sets the bounds of my map to display all markers included within the map.

Question 2:

What is the center point (latitude and longitude) of your area of interest?

This may change as I add markers to the map, but it is currently 35.8193077888718, -99.53178000000003, with a zoom of 5.

Question 3:

What style of map (roads, satellite, hybrid, terrain) is appropriate for your map? Why?

The default roads style is appropriate for this map. The markers within the map are businesses or schools. While satelite imagery of these locations might be interesting, it would also take longer to load in the browser.

Question 4:

What is the scale of your map (local, regional, continental, global)? How will this translate into your selection of an appropriate default zoom level for your map?

The scale of my map is regional, so the default zoom level (5) is somewhat low.
