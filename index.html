Actually, I'm a undergraduate student in the UNM Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences. I'm a GIS minor, and student employee of the Earth Data Analysis Center.

I do not have an auntie and uncle in Bel Air, but I am to blame for many of the maps on NMFlood.org. I'm taking this course to learn a few more things about serving up spatial data on maps on the web.
